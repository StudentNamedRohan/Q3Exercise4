Singapore

Singapore is a sovereign island country and city-state in maritime Southeast Asia. It's a city of noy just one island but 64 different offshore island which include Sentosa, Pulua Ubin, St. John's Island and Sister' Islands. It has a man-made waterfall. It pioneered the first F1 night race where it have a spectacular night views of Singapore. it is one of the greenest cities in the world and home to a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

What dto do in Singapore: Visit the Merlion, Go to Singapore Zoo, Go to Universal Studio Singapore, See the Marina Bay Sands, Go to the Gardens by the Bay, Go to Jewel Changi Airport to see the waterfall, Do a boat ride on the Merlion Park, Go to Mint Museum of Toys or Go to Sentosa to relax there.

Singapore have their own airlines, so if you want to go there, you can use Singapore Airlines to get your ticket to visit Singapore.
